# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Two audiences, one page:
- **Recruiters / hiring managers in marketing & data** — skim quickly to judge whether Kantaphon is worth interviewing. They want proof of real skills (Social Listening, data insight, content) fast.
- **Business clients & partners** — evaluating Kantaphon for project work, business development, or collaboration. They look for credibility, results, and a sense of how he thinks.

Both arrive with low patience and high skepticism, often on mobile, and decide in seconds whether to keep scrolling.

## Product Purpose

A personal portfolio for **Kantaphon Aochampa** (Marketing & Business Development, data-driven marketer). It exists to **showcase work and capabilities** — competitions, certificates, work experience, and detailed case studies (e.g. TCG Social Listening research) — so a visitor leaves convinced he can turn data into insight and insight into impact. The site is also self-managed: a built-in admin CMS (admin.html → localStorage) lets Kantaphon edit content, work pieces, and section backgrounds without touching code. Success = a visitor understands his strengths and remembers him.

## Brand Personality

**Investigative · Observant · Sharp.** The voice is a detective-analyst (a nod to the Detective Conan theme): someone who notices what others miss, follows the evidence, and explains findings with clarity and quiet confidence. Warm and human, never stiff or corporate. Emotional goal: trust plus a spark of curiosity — "this person sees the signal in the noise."

## Anti-references

- **Generic portfolio templates** — the interchangeable hero + 3 cards + contact form that could belong to anyone. Identity must be unmistakable.
- **Cluttered / cramped layouts** — too much packed in, no breathing room.
- **Overly corporate / stiff** — rigid, formal, lifeless agency-speak.
- **Childish / cartoonish** — the Conan influence is a sophisticated detective *mood* (red-blue-black, investigative framing), never costumey, kawaii, or kiddie.

## Design Principles

1. **Evidence over claims.** Show the work, the data, the result — like a detective laying out proof — rather than asserting adjectives about oneself.
2. **Signal over noise.** Every element earns its place; ruthless focus and breathing room beat density. Clutter is the enemy.
3. **The observant analyst.** Surface the *thinking* behind the work (how an insight was found), not just the output — that's the differentiator.
4. **Confident, not loud.** Sharp and self-assured, but calm; let restraint and craft carry authority instead of decoration.
5. **Personality as seasoning.** The detective/Conan motif is subtle flavor (palette, framing, a few deliberate touches), never a costume that overwhelms the content.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target **WCAG 2.1 AA**: body text ≥ 4.5:1 contrast (critical on the dark theme and over background images — verify text over photo/overlay), large text ≥ 3:1, visible keyboard focus states, and a `prefers-reduced-motion` alternative for every animation. Dark-first theme; ensure the red (#e11d2e) / blue (#3b82f6) accents stay legible on black and aren't the sole signal for meaning.
